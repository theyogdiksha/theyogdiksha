About Us

🙏 Namaste! Yog Diksha is founded by Diksha Mathur, a certified yoga teacher with 3 years of teaching experience and a National Rifle Shooter. Her journey unites the discipline of sports with the depth of yoga, guiding individuals to build strength, clarity, and resilience.

At Yog Diksha, we bring together traditional wisdom, modern approaches, and science-based yoga techniques. This unique blend makes each session authentic, practical, and empowering—supporting healing, balance, and transformation.

We welcome people of all ages and backgrounds, offering both personalized one-on-one sessions and group classes. Our mission is to inspire harmony in body, mind, and spirit, and to help individuals carry the essence of yoga into everyday life.
